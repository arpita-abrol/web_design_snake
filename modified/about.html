Welcome to Slither

Slither is based off of the classic arcade game Snake.

To play, use your arrow keys(up, down, left, right).

To pause, press the spacebar.

At the top of the page, a nunber of options are available. You can select a theme from the drop-down menu, or select the difficuly you would like to play at.

During the game, a number of boosters may appear. Each booster has a unique affect. However, not all boosters are good or bad! Some of them will randomly change if they are an upgrade or downgrade, and you won't know until you pick that booster.
